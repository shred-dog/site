Snowboarding is magical.

There’s something about standing at the top of the hill (or mountain, or handrail), staring down your run, taking it all in, giving your bindings one last reassuring click, that makes you feel alive. It’s the reason you come back again and again–braving steep mountain roads, cold weather, bumps and bruises along the way.


This is why I created Shred Dog. The generation of snowboarding legends I looked up to growing up are passing the torch, and Shred Dog exists to help preserve the essence of snowboarding and all the things that make it magical.
